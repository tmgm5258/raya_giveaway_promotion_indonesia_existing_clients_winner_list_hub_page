6 Campaign Analysis
Key insights from the campaign

📊 Participation & Eligibility

18 existing clients registered, but only 3 (16.7%) earned at least 1 chance. 15 clients registered but failed to meet the tandem deposit + trading requirement. The biggest drop-off factor was zero activity — 10 clients had $0 net deposit and 0 lots traded. Notable near-misses: EVA RAHAYU deposited $6,370 but traded 0 lots; Ferdiansyah Idris traded 195 lots but had $0 net deposit.

🎫 Chance Concentration

38 total chances were held by only 3 eligible clients. R Rindiawan Uji purbowo alone held 63.2% of all chances (24 out of 38). This extreme concentration meant the reward pool of 65 items could only be partially drawn — 27 rewards (41.5%) remained unawarded.

🏆 Reward Distribution

All 38 draws resulted in wins (since each draw removes 1 item from the pool). R Rindiawan received 24 rewards including 4 gold bars and 3 air purifiers. HANG HANS received 13 rewards including 3 gold bars. Ribun Bunyamin received 1 hampers box from his single chance.

💰 Reward Pool Utilisation

Only 58.5% of the reward pool was utilized (38 out of 65 items). Remaining inventory: 22 Hampers Boxes, 3 Gold Bars, 2 Air Purifiers. This under-utilisation is due to the low number of qualifying clients relative to the prize pool size.

👥 Eligibility Barriers

The tandem requirement ($300 deposit AND 3 lots) proved to be a significant barrier. Several clients met one condition but not the other: Linda Gunawan deposited $1,755 but only traded 1.89 lots; Ferdiansyah Idris traded 195 lots but had $0 net deposit. Future campaigns may benefit from adjusted thresholds for the Indonesia existing client segment.

8 Chance Calculation Logic
How lucky draw chances were calculated — based on T&C

Core Formula

Chances = min( floor( Net Deposit ÷ $300 ) , floor( Trading Lots ÷ 3 ) )   [max 65]

Both conditions must be met simultaneously — this is the tandem requirement. The lower of the two determines the chance count. Capped at 65 per client.
